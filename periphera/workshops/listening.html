Boat Laboratory

Kati Hyyppä (FI) and Niklas Roy (DE)

Join us at the Boat Laboratory Workshop to make the water vehicle of your wildest dreams! We have lots of DIY materials, small motors and a big, inflatable pool for prototyping, testing - and eventually racing. You can work solo or team up with a friend. Non-electric boat models, submarines and artistic interpretations of the theme are also welcome. No previous experience is required, just a bit of curiosity, creativity, and a sense of adventure!

Biography

Kati Hyyppä (FI) and Niklas Roy (DE) are Berlin-based artists and tinkerers who create playful, participatory installations and workshops exploring science, technology, and craft. Rooted in DIY and hacker culture, their practice invites curiosity, experimentation, and hands-on engagement. Their works often have a humorous twist, and have been presented at exhibitions and festivals worldwide.
