Art in the age of AI

In the age of artificial intelligence, the world of art is changing quickly. AI can now create its own paintings, music, and even write stories by studying large amounts of data and learning from different styles. This new ability challenges us to think about what it means to be an artist.

Is AI now a tool we use, or has it become a true creative partner? Can AI-driven tools enhance traditional art to create a more immersive experience? Does AI-generated art diminish the value of traditional art? Is it possible for AI to evoke the same emotional conection as human-created art?

While this new technology pushes the boundaries, it also invites us to reflect on what makes art truly meaningful. Join me in my keynote as we navigate this new landscape together and embrace the challenge of balancing what AI has to offer with preserving the human touch that makes art unique.
