To the Edge of the Galaxy

To the Edge of the Galaxy is a live performance combining contemporary dance, improvisation, realtime pose tracking and a game engine, to deliver a whimsical visual narrative set in space. Taking human form, a constellation breaks free and dances its way to the edge of the galaxy. We accompany this entity’s journey and its encounters throughout the cosmos.
