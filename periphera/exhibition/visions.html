Visions of Destruction
Varvara & Mar (EE, ES)
Interactive installation (Stable Diffusion, eyetracking) | 2023

“Visions of Destruction” is an interactive AI work where the viewer’s gaze reshapes idyllic, AI-generated landscapes, inscribing nature with human presence. Using eye-tracking and Stable Diffusion in real time, it inpaints what you look at into scenes of ruin, then slowly regenerates. Echoing Surrealist cut-and-collage and a dreamworld psyche, the piece reveals fragile ecologies and turns looking into a choice - an urgent call for environmental care.

Biography

Varvara & Mar is an artist duo formed in 2009 by Varvara Guljajeva and Mar Canet Sola. Their practice explores how art, technology, and society intersect, engaging AI, robotics, and interactivity. They have exhibited at MAD, FACT, and Ars Electronica, and present research at SIGGRAPH and ISEA. Varvara is Associate Professor of Kinetic Imaging at VCUarts Qatar; Mar is a substitute professor at KHM Cologne and a research fellow at Tallinn University and Max Planck Institute, Berlin.
