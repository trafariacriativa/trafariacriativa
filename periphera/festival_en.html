THE FESTIVAL

What is Periphera?

The PERIPHERA festival will take place on the South Bank between the 8th and 10th of November, divided between the Convento dos Capuchos and Trafaria. It will present a set of hybrid initiatives between art and technology, questioning and challenging this connection.

The PERIPHERA Festival aims to encourage creativity and raise awareness of trends in technological and artistic environments, inspiring the creation and knowledge of the plastic and aesthetic potential provided by the combination of these media.

It is based on values ​​and principles of

  • Creativity
  • Democratization
  • Participation
  • Innovation
  • Access
  • Education
  • Discovery/curiosity
  • Inclusion

The Periphera Festival has the following objectives:

  • Involve young people and local residents, promoting the use of technologies and their potential in expression, communication and employability.
  • Transform spaces and inspire new ideas.
  • Contribute to establishing Almada and Trafaria as a reference in art and technology.
  • Give a stage to local talents and promote networking.
  • Create a lasting platform that connects local reality with the global world.
  • Offer space for creators and artists to showcase their ideas.
  • Facilitate partnerships and cooperation.
  • Contribute to the creative and technological development of the region.
