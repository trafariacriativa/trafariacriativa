WORKSHOPS

Initiation to Musical Creation

Trafaria | October 9th | 2 pm

This workshop is aimed at anyone who wants to take their first steps in creating music using modular systems. We will experiment with generative systems, musical compositions, sound effects, logical operations, sequencing, quantization, among many other musical composition concepts, always within an open architecture system.

Introduction to Touchdesigner

NOVA FCT | October 15th | 10 am

TouchDesigner is a real-time generative creation platform, enabling you to create of immersive and interactive visual experiences.
In this hands-on workshop, we'll explore the infinite possibilities for visual creation that TouchDesigner offers us, exploring generative animations, interactivity, 2D and 3D content, among other topics.

Introduction to 3D Printing of Cultural Heritage

Trafaria | November 8th | 10 hours and November 10th | 10.30 hours

In this two-day training, we will explore the essential basic concepts to get started with 3D printing. As a practical component, we will print objects from museum collections that you can take home!

Getting started with Makecode Arcade

Trafaria | November 9th | 10 am

Makecode Arcade is the ideal tool to introduce children to the world of programming.
This workshop is a fun and challenging activity that can help children develop their logical and critical thinking skills, allowing them to create their own projects and games, and take their ideas into the real world.

Introduction to Application Development in Unity with Visual Programming

Trafaria | November 10th | 15.30 hours

In this workshop, we will learn how to develop applications in Unity using visual programming, an intuitive approach that allows for creating interactive experiences without the need for prior programming knowledge.
