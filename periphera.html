From November 8th to 10th, the Trafaria Art and Technology Festival, Periphera, will take place at the Convento dos Capuchos and in Trafaria. The festival proposes a reflection on the intersections between art and technology, promoting creativity and inclusion over three days of exhibitions, performances, talks, workshops, and demonstrations of projects and companies.
