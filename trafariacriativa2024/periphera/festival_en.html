THE FESTIVAL

From November 8th to 10th, the Trafaria Art and Technology Festival, Periphera, will take place at the Convento dos Capuchos and in Trafaria. The festival proposes a reflection on the intersections between art and technology, promoting creativity and inclusion over three days of exhibitions, performances, talks, workshops, and demonstrations of innovative projects and companies.

The combination of experiences around the intersection of art and technology serves as a starting point for sharing creation processes, new working methods, and ways to have an impact on local and global communities. Technology offers new artistic possibilities, and art, in turn, can transcend the functionality of technological creations, provoking surprise, reflection, and opening new perspectives.

The talks and workshops will address topics ranging from the use of new technologies in creative contexts to how art can be a tool for social and cultural reflection. The festival is an opportunity for the public to experience these connections in an accessible and interactive way.

The demonstrations of projects and companies offer a practical perspective on ongoing innovations, focusing on initiatives that unite technological development with artistic practice.

Held in a historic setting like the Convento dos Capuchos, in harmony with the atmosphere of Trafaria, the festival creates a unique environment for these encounters. Through a diverse program, it aims to open space for new ideas and collaborations, reflecting on the impact of technology on the future of artistic practices and everyday life.
