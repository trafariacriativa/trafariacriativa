Fragments of an Entropy

Between September and December 2022, choreographers Marina Nabais and Ricardo Machado created the performance *ENTROPIA* at the São Domingos Mine, at the invitation of the MALACATE project. This video brings together filmed notes from the creation process.

With Camila Duarte, Ivone Gomes, Maria José Baptista, Geraldine Zwanikken, Sophia Rukosueva, Pedro Nogueira, Marina Nabais, Ricardo Machado Based on an idea by Marina Nabais and Ricardo Machado Image and Editing by Gonçalo Mota Music by Rémi Gallet with Ana Santos Fashion “Lindo Ramo, Verde Escuro” interpreted by José Fabião As part of the MALACATE project.

Directed by CEPATORTA Miguel Maia Production by Leonor Carpinteiro Special thanks to Mina de São Domingos, Fundação Serrão Martins Archive, Terra Sintrópica Association, Montes Altos Social Center, Cláudio Torres, Convento de São Francisco, Horta da Malhadinha, São Domingos Football Club, Serpa Football Club.
