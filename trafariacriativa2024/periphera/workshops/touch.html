Introduction to Touchdesigner

Guilherme Martins

TouchDesigner is a real-time generative creation platform, allowing you to create of immersive and interactive visual experiences. Through its visual programming interface, offers powerful video manipulation, animation, and interaction capabilities, being widely used in artistic installations, live performances, and audiovisual production.

This workshop is aimed at anyone who wants to take their first steps in creating visuals generative and interactive designs with powerful TouchDesigner software.

During this practical workshop, we will explore infinite visual creation possibilities that the TouchDesigner offers us, exploring generative animations, interactivity, 2D content and 3D, among other topics.

Workshop Content

  • Introduction to TouchDesigner, interface and different operators
  • Networks and Basic Principles of Node-based Programming
  • Graphics animation
  • Video Manipulation
  • Interaction Design
  • Export and Performance

Workshop duration

The nature of this workshop allows its duration to be adaptable, however there is a minimum duration of 2 hours.

Maximum participants

12 to 15 people

Ages

12 to 99.

If participants are younger, they must be accompanied by someone to help them.

Requirements

  • Computer (windows, mac)
  • Mouse
  • Headphones
  • If possible, bring the TouchDesigner software already installed.

Note*

Touchdesigner is demanding on computer resources and processing is done in GPU (graphics card).
The computer must be from 2016 or newer, with a dedicated graphics card.
Minimum 4GB Ram, 1GB free disk space.
If the computer does not meet these requirements, the performance of the Touchdesigner program will be reduced. limited and may not even work as desired.
