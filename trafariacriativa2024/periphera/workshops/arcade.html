Getting started with programming with Makecode Arcade

Guilherme Martins

Learning to program is important for everyone, especially for children because Programming helps you develop valuable skills such as problem solving, creativity, logic and mathematics. Programming can also help children better understand how technologies work, and develop an understanding criticism of the social and environmental impacts of technology.

This workshop is a fun and challenging activity that can help children develop their logical and critical thinking capacity, allowing them to create their own own projects and games, and transfer your ideas into the real world.

Makecode Arcade is the ideal tool to introduce children to the world of schedule. Through blocks that drag and group together, it is possible to create simple games, but also make complex games, with various difficulty levels, different objectives and much more.

No prior programming knowledge is required, everyone is welcome.

In this workshop we will play a simple game of catch, with three characters, the main character who has to catch a second character, at the same time as you have to escape from a third character. This game will have scores and lives.

Workshop Duration

1 hour

Maximum Number of Participants

8 to 10 participants

Ages

6 to 12 years old

Younger participants should be accompanied by an adult.

Necessary Equipment Each Participant Should Bring

Laptop.
